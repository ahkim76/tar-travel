<p>Founded in 1928, Aarhus University is the largest and second oldest research university in Denmark, boasting an enrollment total of over 44,000. AU is ranked as one of world's top 100 universities, and offers a wide array of courses taught in English. The university's modern, yellow-brick campus, which is located in the center of downtown Aarhus, is often considered to be one of the most beautiful campuses in Europe.</p>

<h4><strong>Location</strong></h4>
</td><td>
<p><strong>Aarhus, Denmark</strong></p>
<p>Aarhus, the country’s second biggest city, is often considered one of the best places to study in Denmark. Aarhus is situated on the East coast of the Jutland Peninsula, and living in the city provides an immersive view into Danish life and culture.</p>
<p>Aarhus is the cultural and economic core of Denmark's Jutland region, and is the region's largest center for trade, services, tourism and industry. Aarhus is especially well-known for it's musical history, and offers a vibrant music scene, with several large-scale music festivals hosted every year in the city.</p>
