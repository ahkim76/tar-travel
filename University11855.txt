<p>This program is available for <strong><a href="https://studyabroad.unc.edu/find-program/program-types-search/#3mlbc7865433-393-7841">exchange</a>. </strong></p>
<p><strong>This exchange program is open to graduate students who have been accepted into the UNC School of Information and Library Science. </strong></p>
<p><strong>The language prerequisite is 4 semesters, completed by the start of the program.  </strong></p>
<p>Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile was originally founded in 1888. Since then, it has grown to one of South America’s premier universities with over 30,000 students, 18 faculties and 4 campuses spread across Chile’s capital city, Santiago.   </p>
<p>Please note that the first semester at UC Chile runs from March to July and the second semester runs from August to December.   </p>
